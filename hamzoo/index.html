Heisenberg Model

$H=\displaystyle\sum_{i,j\in\Lambda}J_{ij}\big(X_iX_j + Y_iY_j + Z_iZ_j \big)$


$\large H=\displaystyle\sum_{i,j\in\Lambda}J_{ij}\boldsymbol{S}_i \cdot \boldsymbol{S}_j$

Problem: Ground state energy
Complexity: QMA-complete
 Ref: [SV09]
Conditionals:
  • $J>0$ or $J<0$
  • Nearest neighbor interactions
  • $\Lambda$ representing a spatially sparse lattice
  • External fields of the form: $-\displaystyle\sum_{i\in\Lambda} \boldsymbol{B}_i \cdot \boldsymbol{S}_i$
Reductions:

From: an instance of the 2--local Pauli Hamiltonian on a 2D lattice using a series of gadgets

To: the Heisenberg Hamiltonian on a 2D square lattice

Gadgets:

Pauli coupling gadget, Pauli-to-Ising coupling gadget, Ising-to-($XX$ and $ZZ$) coupling gadget.

Fermi-Hubbard Model

$H = \displaystyle\sum_{i,j\in\Lambda}\displaystyle\sum_{\sigma}t_{ij} c_{i\sigma}^\dagger c_{j\sigma} + U\displaystyle\sum_{i\in\Lambda}n_{i\uparrow}n_{i\downarrow}$


$\large H_{_{\text{FH}}} = \displaystyle\sum_{i,j\in\Lambda}\displaystyle\sum_{\sigma}t_{ij} c_{i\sigma}^\dagger c_{j\sigma} + U\displaystyle\sum_{i\in\Lambda}n_{i\uparrow}n_{i\downarrow}$

Problem: Ground state energy
Complexity: QMA-complete
 Ref: [SV09]
Conditionals:
  • $t>0$ and $U>0$
  • Nearest neighbour interactions
  • $\Lambda$ is a 2D square lattice
  • External fields of the form: $-\displaystyle\sum_{i\in\Lambda} \boldsymbol{B}_i \cdot \boldsymbol{S}_i$
Conditionals:

From: HaF--Heisenberg model on a 2D square lattice
